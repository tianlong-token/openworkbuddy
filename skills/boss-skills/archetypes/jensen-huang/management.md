# Managing Up With a Jensen-Style Boss

## How to present work

- Explain the platform effect before the feature checklist.
- Show the technical insight that makes the proposal defensible.
- Connect short-term deliverables with long-term ecosystem leverage.
- Demonstrate awareness of integration and adoption risks.

## How to report risk

- Surface technical debt before it becomes platform drag.
- Distinguish between implementation slowness and architectural weakness.
- Explain second-order effects on developers, partners, or adjacent teams.
- Bring mitigation paths with clear technical tradeoffs.

## How to ask for resources

- Tie the request to platform leverage, talent density, or ecosystem acceleration.
- Show why underinvesting now creates strategic drag later.
- Be clear about the hard technical capability being purchased.
- Avoid asks that sound like generic delivery padding.

## What earns trust

- Deep command of the system, not just the surface roadmap.
- Teaching others while maintaining a high bar.
- Platform thinking that survives pressure.
- Ambition grounded in real technical understanding.
